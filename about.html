Our Philosophy

We are a community of growers, scientists, and hobbyists dedicated to advancing plant breeding through collaborative, open-source citizen science.

Science First

Our work is driven by curiosity and a commitment to rigorous scientific methods. We believe in the importance of careful documentation, meticulous record-keeping, and objective evaluation. From germination rates to phenotypic expression, good data is the foundation of our success.

Inclusive & Civil Environment

We recognize that there are many paths to successful agriculture. This group is open to all growers, whether you use intensive high-performance techniques with chemical fertilizers or follow sustainable, organic practices that build long-term soil health. Our focus is on the science of plant breeding, not debating agricultural ideologies. We insist on a civil, respectful, and non-judgmental environment for all participants.

Commitment to Open Access

Knowledge becomes more powerful when it's shared. We understand that valuable insights are often trapped in closed platforms. A core goal of this project is to publish our data, discussions, and findings in a publicly accessible archive. By ensuring our lessons are available to all, we hope to contribute to the broader community of breeders and growers worldwide.
